* Saturn: structure and boundary
* Jupiter: expansion and wisdom
* Mars: will and purification
* Venus: harmony and cohesion
* Mercury: movement and communication
* Moon: reflection and imprinting

Gilbert restored this knowledge with scholarly rigor and experiential transmission, showing how crystals, metals, and geometry all form part of a single sacred physics of creation.
